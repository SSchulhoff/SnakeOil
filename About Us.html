Welcome to GalaxiesRus

We are a small locally owned non-profit created in 2014. We are located at 801 W 36th St. We were originally created to help cancer patients and are proud to say we have donated over $5,000,000 to cancer patients across the country. Our goal is to reach $10,000,000 donated by 2030. Please help us reach this goal by buying or renaming a galaxy. You can find the available galaxies on the page called Image Gallery.
